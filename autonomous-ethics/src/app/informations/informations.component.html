People's opinion gathering system for autonomous vehicles decisions in life-threatening situations

Christophe RUIZ

What is it ?

The purpose of this website is to collect people's opinion and build statistics to provide information on how certain groups of people would react if they were to choose which lives should be saved in a dangerous situation.

The situation described is a sudden break failure on a self-driving car.

The user embodies the judge of the situation and has to choose which way to go so the outcome is more acceptable according to the user's opinion.

How is it useful ?

In my opinion, this kind of devices could be used to prepare the arrival of SAE level 4 and 5 self-driving vehicles on streets. It could help gather people's opinion in order to prepare laws and rules that these vehicles and devices should follow when suffering a critical failure.

It can also help identify how certain groups of people value people's life according to their social status.

How do you use it ?

To provide your opinion to the system you can click the "Start Judging" button in the header.

You will then have to fill in anonymous informations about you in order to correctly link your answers to your social traits.

This will allow you to access a session of 10 choices and publish your results to the database.
